📜 Community Guidelines

⚠️ Violations will result in suspensions starting from 24 hours up to permanent ban

🚫 Prohibited Behavior

• Harassment, bullying, or threatening others

• Nudity, sexual content, or explicit material

• Hate speech or discrimination

• Spam or disruptive behavior

🎯 Expected Conduct

• Treat others with respect

• Stay on topic in themed rooms

• Report violations using red flag button

🔒 Privacy & Data

• Conversations are peer-to-peer and not recorded

• Reports are reviewed by AI and moderators

• Your data is never sold to third parties
